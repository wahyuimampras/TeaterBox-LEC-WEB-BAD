Matilda The Musical - Sinopsis

"Roald Dahl's Matilda the Musical" stars: Alisha Weir as Matilda Wormwood, an extraordinary little girl with a sharp mind and a vivid imagination who dares to take a stand to change her story. The film also features Emma Thompson as the tyrannical headmistress, Miss Trunchbull; Lashana Lynch as her inspirational teacher, Miss Honey; and Stephen Graham and Andrea Riseborough as Matilda's crass and uncaring parents, Mr. and Mrs. Wormwood. Guided by Miss Honey, Matilda discovers her superpowers and uses them to stand up to the tyrannical Miss Trunchbull for the good of all.
