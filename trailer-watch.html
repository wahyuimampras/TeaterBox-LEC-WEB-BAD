Matilda The Musical - Sinopsis

"Matilda the Musical" tells the story of Matilda Wormwood, an exceptionally brilliant and imaginative girl trapped by her crass, uncaring family. Upon attending school, she finds a kind-hearted ally in her teacher, Miss Honey, but also faces the tyrannical and child-hating headmistress, Miss Trunchbull. Discovering she has the power of telekinesis, Matilda bravely uses her unique gifts to stand up to the cruel adults in her life and rewrite her own destiny.
